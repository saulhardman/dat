BA/BSc (Hons) Digital Art and Technology

With a 20 year history, Digital Art and Technology at Plymouth University is one of the UK’s best and most established interactive media degrees.

Our graduates are amongst the world’s best game designers, digital artists, web designers, coders and creative thinkers.

The course combines creative and technical skills with cultural and new media theory - producing graduates who are highly employable while also seeing the world in a new way.

We aim to future-proof our students, to prepare them for a always changing world, where technologies change, but smart thinking is always in demand.

Breaking down barriers between disciplines, the course can be taken as either a BSc or a BA. “Messing with the future” is our mantra.
